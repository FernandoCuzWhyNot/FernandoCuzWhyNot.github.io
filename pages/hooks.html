Hook Functions Information

Although you're probably a player from Project: SCP trying to find out about an updated and reliable source of information about Hook Functions, I'll still do a small explanation of what they are. Hook Functions are a way for Community Server Owners to customize their servers along with custom maps and custom rooms to give a unique experience. Hook Functions are in "lox" language, although their functions are internally made with LUA. Here you will find the documentation about functions compatible with Hook Functions.
